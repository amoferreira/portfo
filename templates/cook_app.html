Cook Assistant

Cooking units converter mobile app. The app is used to convert mass and volume units (16 units allowed) for 6 different ingredients. It also contains an interface that allows temperature conversion.

This project was developed under the scope of the curricular unit Mobile Computing in Biomedical Engineering in the 2nd year of the master in Bioengineering (2021).
